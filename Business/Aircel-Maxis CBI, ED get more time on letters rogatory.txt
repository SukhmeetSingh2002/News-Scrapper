Aircel-Maxis: CBI, ED get more time on letters rogatory

TNN |  Feb 3, 2021, 06:37  IST

NEW DELHI: A special CBI court has granted ED and CBI time till February 26 to obtain reports on letters rogatory (LRs) from the UK and other nations in connection with its ongoing probe in the Aircel-Maxis case against former Union minister P Chidambaram and his son Karti. Special judge MK Nagpal was informed that besides the UK, responses to LRs are awaited from Singapore. It was also told that Malaysian authorities had refused to give information.
